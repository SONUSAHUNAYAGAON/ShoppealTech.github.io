Shoppeal E-learning includes Shoppeal VIP (Virtual Internship Program). It operates on a subscription basis. There will be monthly batches, and this programme is divided into two segments. The first will be free, while the second will be paid one.

Perks & Benefits (Free Summer Virtual Internship):

  • 1. Verified Completion certificate
  • 2. Certificate of Excellence
  • 3. Letter of Recommendations (Performance Based)
  • 4. Hands on work experience
  • 5. Opportunity to work in collaboration with partner companies worldwide.
  • 6. Insight into future career paths

Learn More
Perks & Benefits (Paid Summer Virtual Internship):

  • 1. Verified Completion certificate
  • 2. Letter of Appreciations & Recommendation
  • 3. Google meetups for one-on-one mentoring
  • 4. Live training & doubt clearing session
  • 5. Become an intern in the Shoppeal core team (Performance Based)
  • 6. Will aid in building resume & obtaining a full-time position

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Shoppeal VIP?

Shoppeal VIP is a part of Shoppeal E-learning programme that provides Virtual Internship Program to the students.

2. How Shoppeal VIP is different from others?

Shoppeal VIP is a digital solution that provides students with career-relevant tech programmes by delivering highly flexible, attractive, and effective learning solutions

3. What are the different programmes offered in Shoppeal VIP?

We are offering two programmes in monthly batches. The programmes are: Free Summer Virtual Internship & Paid Summer Virtual Internshi

4. Will I get a certificate post internship?

Yes, upon successful completion of your internship you will be awarded with verified certificate of completion.

5. Is there any provision of letter of recommendation?

Yes, we provide letter of recommendation to students who perform extra ordinarily during their internship.

6. What are the benefits of paid internship?

We provide 1-to-1 mentorship to the students through live training & doubt clearing sessions. We also provide aid in building resumes & obtaining a full-time position.

7. How do I contact my course instructor?

Students get the chance to interact with their mentors through google meets.

8. How qualified are the mentors?

Mentors are working experts in the tech field who will help students build real-world essential skills.
